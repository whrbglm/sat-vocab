Squad A — The Dimmer Switch
Words that amplify or diminish. ON = intensify / highlight. OFF = weaken / conceal.
ON — Amplify
compelling substantiate emphasize underscore accentuate validates corroborates
OFF — Diminish
negligible peripheral cursory superficial rudimentary nullify mitigate
Squad B — Truth Seekers
Words about what's real or fake. Real = verifiable. Fake = misleading/false.
REAL — Verify
empirical plausible definitive explicit rigorous
FAKE — Mislead
speculative implicit disconfirmation inadvertently dogmatic
Squad C — The Game Changers
Change = transformation/replacement. Stay = preservation/stagnation.
CHANGE — Transform
unprecedented transformative pioneering innovative revolutionary
STAY — Preserve
perpetuates preserve adherence conventional resilient
Squad D — The Emotion Map
Mixed = nuanced or conflicted feelings. Extreme = intense emotions at either end.
MIXED — Nuanced
dissonance frisson evasive speculate
EXTREME — Intense
exuberant impassioned pitiless labyrinthine conspicuous
